About The Weeknd

Abel Tesfaye, also known as The Weeknd, was born on February 16th, 1990. Abel was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario. His music career started in 2011 when he released his first mixtape, House of Balloons, and he has risen to fame ever since. He released his second album, Kiss Land, in 2013, then The Beauty Behind The Madness in 2015, which won a grammy not long after. Starboy was released in 2015, After Hours in 2020, then his newest release, Dawn FM, in 2022. Thanks to the success of After Hours, The Weeknd got to perform at the Super-bowl Halftime show. Abel is one of the most listen to artists worldwide, and he continues to thank his supporters for helping his dreams come true.
