About The Weeknd

His music career started in 2011 when he released his first mixtape, House of Balloons, and he has risen to fame ever since. He released his second album, Kiss Land, in 2013, then The Beauty Behind The Madness in 2015, which won a grammy not long after. Starboy was released in 2015, After Hours in 2020, then his newest release, Dawn FM, in 2022.
